While performing onsite inspection, it is recommended
that the surveyor emphasises on the below points:
  • Based on our records, the location has 40% higher
    probability of water damage. It would be strongly
    recommended that the pipelines are thoroughly
    checked.
  • Based on past records building made of wood and roof
    made of asphalt have 20% higher  chance of a claim
    compared to other construction and roof types. Hence it
    would be recommended that the inspector pay extra
    attention to the quality/stability of the structure.Past
    records Indicate 10% higher probability of foundation
    damage due to tree roots overgrowth.
  • Past records Indicate 10% higher probability of
    foundation damage due to tree roots overgrowth.
It is recommended that the inspector pays extra attention
to any tree roots that might grow near the building
foundation.
